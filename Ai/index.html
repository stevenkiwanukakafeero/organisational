What is The AI Goal?

To create intelligent systems that can solve real-world problems efficiently and improve human lives
through automation, innovation, and enhanced decision-making.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think, learn, and make decisions like humans. AI enables machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as understanding natural language, recognizing patterns, solving problems, and making decisions

Origin Of Ai

AI's roots lie in ancient myths of intelligent machines but formally began at the 1956 Dartmouth Conference, where "AI" was coined. Pioneers like Turing and McCarthy laid its foundations with concepts like the Turing Test and the Lisp language, driving efforts to emulate human intelligence
